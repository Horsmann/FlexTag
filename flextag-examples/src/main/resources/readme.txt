The annotated data are take from Alan Ritter et al.: Named Entity Recognition in Tweets: An Experimental Study (2011), http://www.aclweb.org/anthology/D11-1141 
The full data is available at https://github.com/aritter/twitter_nlp (retrieved, 22. Feb 2016)

The folder 
	train/
	test/
contain only a small fraction of the training data provided by Ritter.

The brown cluster are those created by Owoputi et al.: Part-of-Speech Tagging for Twitter: Word Clusters and Other Advances (2012), http://www.cs.cmu.edu/~ark/TweetNLP/owoputi+etal.tr12.pdf
Downloaded from: http://www.cs.cmu.edu/~ark/TweetNLP/clusters/50mpaths2
